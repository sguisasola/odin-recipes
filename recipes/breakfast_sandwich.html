Vegan Breakfast Sandwich

The Vegan Breakfast Sandwich is an absolutely delicious sandwich that can be enjoyed at any time of the day. It is truly a vegan culinary masterpiece.

Ingredients

  • Vegan English muffin
  • Vegan cheddar cheese slice
  • JUST egg patty
  • Beyond Meat spicy breakfast sausage patty

Preparation

  1. Toast the vegan English muffin.
  2. Place the JUST egg patty and a Beyond Meat breakfast sausage patty into an air fryer for 8 minutes at 375F.
  3. Assemble the sandwich.
  4. Enjoy this delectable bread-bound meal!

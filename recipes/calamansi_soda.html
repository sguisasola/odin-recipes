Calamansi Soda

The Calamansi Soda is an absolutely delicious drink that can be enjoyed at any time of the day. It is truly a drinkable masterpiece.

Ingredients

  • Calamansi juice or calamansi
  • Sparkling water

Preparation

  1. Squeeze the calamansi using a citrus squeezer to extract its delicious juice. (Skip this step if the calamansi has already been juiced.)
  2. Mix the calamansi juice with sparkling water at a ratio of 1:10.
  3. Enjoy this exhilarating refreshment!

Tofu Scramble

The Tofu Scramble is an absolutely delicious dish that can be enjoyed at any time of the day. You can eat it with toast, as pictured to the left, or by itself. It is truly a vegan culinary masterpiece.

Ingredients

  • Firm tofu
  • Nutritional yeast
  • Turmeric
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Smoked paprika
  • Olive oil

Preparation

  1. Fork the tofu or cut it into cubes after draining. Put it into a bowl.
  2. Add all spices to taste to the bowl and mix.
  3. Add the olive oil to a pan on medium heat.
  4. Cook the tofu for five minutes, mixing often.
  5. Enjoy this delectable refection!
